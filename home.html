Rainworld
Survivor Campaign guide

Rainworld is a game in which you play as a nomadic slug cat traversing through an extremely harsh unforgiving environment, full of countless deadly creatures that can easily kill you if you're not being extremely careful. Rainworld is a brilliant game, the game barely explains any of its mechanics, which leaves many players confused and frustrated, resulting in them likely giving up the game. This website will provide you with a basic guide on the Survivor campaign in Rainworld, giving information on various creatures and regions.
